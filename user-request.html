Document Request Service

Certificates Available ▼
  • Barangay Certificate
  • Certificate of Indigency
  • Proof of Residency
  • Local Employment
  • Barangay Business Permit
  • Barangay ID
  • First Time Job Seeker Certificate
Procedure ▼
  1. Step 1: Fill out the request form with accurate information.
  2. Step 2: Submit the form and wait for confirmation.
  3. Step 3: Pay the required fees at the barangay office.
  4. Step 4: Wait for processing (usually takes 3-5 business days).
  5. Step 5: Pick up your document at the barangay office.
Guidelines in Claiming Requested Documents ▼
  • Bring a valid ID when claiming documents
  • Documents can be claimed within 30 days from approval
  • Claiming hours: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM, Monday to Friday
  • Authorized representatives must present authorization letter
  • Processing time: 3-5 business days
